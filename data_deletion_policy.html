User Data Deletion for Wowlyx

If you wish to delete your account or personal data associated with Wowlyx:

  1. Send an email to wowlyx@thopps.com with the subject line: Data Deletion Request.
  2. Include the email address linked to your account.
  3. Your data will be permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days of your request.

Alternatively, you may contact us through the in-app support form (if available).
